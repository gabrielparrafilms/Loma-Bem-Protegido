Body Text Roles

font-family: 'Inter', sans-serif;

Body Large (text-lg, font-normal, leading-relaxed)

This is the large body text used for subtitles and introductory paragraphs. It provides high legibility at larger sizes.

Body Default (text-base, font-normal, text-zinc-400)

The standard body text. Used for main content, articles, and general interface text where comfortable reading is essential.

Body Small (text-sm, font-normal, text-zinc-500)

Small text used for captions, metadata, and secondary information that shouldn't compete with main content.
